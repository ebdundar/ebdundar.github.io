Scholar Linkedin Twitter GitHub

I am a Senior Research Engineer NLP/AI at Findest B.V., Amsterdam, Netherlands. I have completed my MSc. Thesis under the supervision of Prof. Ethem Alpaydın, at Boğaziçi University. I have focused on deep learning architectures for Turkish. Moreover, our work has been published in a conference. While I was an undergraduate student, at Yeditepe University, I had conducted several productive studies on cellular automata with Prof. Emin Erkan Korkmaz. And, my BSc. studies were then published in two journals and a conference.

interested in

Natural Language Processing

Both of my academic and professional experiences are highly related to several topics of NLP, from modeling conversational agents to parsing CVs or resumes.

Cellular Automata

It is a quite interesting topic to study on. Furthermore, my contributions on this topic are on supervised and unsupervised learning.

Reinforcement Learning

RL always attracts my attention. To extend my knowledge on this topic, I took a course given by NVIDIA on Udacity. Projects which I did for this course can be found on my GitHub respository.
